Last Updated: 11-Sep-24

Lybell Graphchain Ltd ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or subscribe to our mailing list.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your email address to outside parties except as necessary for the operation of our mailing list services.

5. Your Rights

  • Unsubscribe: You can unsubscribe from our mailing list at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of our emails.
  • Access and Correction: You have the right to request access to your personal information and request corrections if it is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Data Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal information from our records by contacting us.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

  • Our website does not use cookies or tracking technologies that collect personal information about you.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
