Graphchain

The Graphchain is a collaborative, decentralised, fast and efficient alternative to the classic blockchain. Built to scale.

The Graphchain operates a DAG-like in the consesnus mechanism, which vastly improves state update and agreement times.

Features

Fast

Extremely-fast transaction speeds, through the DAG-like consensus mechanism

Scalable

Fundamental DAG-like design enables exceptional scalability, accommodating huge parallel transaction volume

Robust

Robust cryptography, capturing and recording even invalid transactions

Green

Eco-friendly design, prioritising energy efficiency and sustainability in consensus

Checkpointed

Graphchain is designed with cross-chain checkpointing, providing a relaible backup of the current state

Easy

Graphchain has low transaction costs and quick and simple ways to get started
