A New Era In Scale, Trust, Decentralisation

Graphchain offers a DAG-based, fully decentralized consensus mechanism, focsuing on creating a scalable, fair and simple alternative to current blockchain systems.

What makes us different?

The whitepaper is formed from academic researchers. Community driven. Mining. Fair-launch. No ICO. No foundation. No team tokens. No advisors. No airdrops. No nonsense.

Graphchain is a fair, collaborative, decentralised, fast and efficient alternative to the classic blockchain structures. It is designed to scale.

Features

Fast

Graphchain provides extremely-fast transaction speeds, through the DAG-like consensus mechanism.

Scalable

Graphchain has a fundamental DAG-like design, which enables exceptional scalability, accommodating huge parallel transaction volume. Graphchain operates a DAG-like consensus mechanism, which vastly improves state update and agreement times.

Robust

Graphchain uses robust cryptographic techniques for validation, transaction creation and consensus.

Green

Graphchain prioritises energy efficiency and sustainability in consensus.

Checkpointed

Graphchain is designed with implicit and explicit (cross-chain) checkpointing, providing a reliable backup of the current state.

Easy

Graphchain has low transaction token costs and quick and simple ways to participate.

Simple

Graphchain has pseudocode, python and rust implementations. Making it as human readable as possible.
