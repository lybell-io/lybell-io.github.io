About Graphchain

Graphchain is a collaborative, decentralized, fast, and efficient alternative to the classic blockchain. It is designed with scalability in mind, operating a DAG-like consensus mechanism to improve state update and agreement times.

Developed by?

Graphchain is developed by Lybell Graphchain Ltd, and will be made Open Source as soon as reasonably possible.
